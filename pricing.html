Frequently Asked Questions

How do I hire a freelancer?

Simply post your job with a clear description and budget. Freelancers will submit proposals, and you can review their profiles, ratings, and past work before hiring the best fit for your project.

How do freelancers submit proposals?

Freelancers can browse available jobs, view details, and submit bids or proposals directly through the platform. They can include a cover note, estimated timeline, and proposed budget.

How does the bidding process work?

Freelancers submit proposals with their rates, timelines, and cover notes. Clients can review multiple bids, compare skills and experience, and choose the best freelancer for their project.

How is payment handled?

Clients pay securely through the platform. Funds are only released to the freelancer after the work is completed and approved, ensuring a safe and reliable transaction for both parties.

Can I negotiate the bid with freelancers?

Absolutely. Clients and freelancers can communicate through the platform’s chat to discuss project scope, timelines, and budgets before finalizing the hire.

Can I communicate with freelancers before hiring?

Yes! The platform offers a built-in chat feature that allows clients and freelancers to discuss project details, clarify requirements, and ensure a good fit before starting work.
