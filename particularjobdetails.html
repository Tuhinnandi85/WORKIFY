Luxury Packaging (Beauty) E-Commerce Design

Hourly · Posted 1 days ago

Experience Level : Expert
Duration : 20 Days
Fixed Price : ₹100 – ₹140
Job Type : Remote
Mandatory skills :
Product Label Packaging Design Bottle & Box Design
Graphic Design Illustration Adobe Photoshop

Project Summary

We are seeking a skilled packaging designer with experience in luxury beauty, fragrance, or skincare brands such as Lux, Dove, Biotique, SebaMed, or Vatika. This project involves designing packaging for a premium pet-care line that meets high-end beauty standards. A well-defined brand strategy and core brand assets are already in place.

Project Scope

The initial launch includes three core products with a total of seven fragrances:

  • Shampoo (400ml)
  • Conditioner (400ml)
  • 2-in-1 (400ml)

The packaging requirements include:

  • Bottle label layouts with clear hierarchy for 400ml bottles (pump or cap top)
  • Outer product box for single units
  • E-commerce shipping box design
  • Information inserts
  • Print-ready files compliant with UAE production standards
  • Material and finish recommendations, including color, embossing, texture, varnish, and laminates

Design Direction

The packaging should convey a minimalist, luxurious, fine-fragrance feel, with clean, understated typography. Fragrance should drive storytelling.

Success Criteria

A successful outcome delivers a cohesive, instantly premium packaging system that:

  • Maintains a unified parent brand identity
  • Works seamlessly across retail shelves and lifestyle photography
  • Clearly communicates luxury and product performance at first glance

Who We’re Looking For

We are seeking a designer with:

  • A strong portfolio in beauty, skincare, or fragrance packaging
  • The ability to deliver clean, production-ready files quickly
  • A keen eye for typography, material selection, and transitioning products from FMCG to luxury
